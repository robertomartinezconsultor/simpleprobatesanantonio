Meet Your Attorney

Dedicated to Simple, Affordable Probate

For over 15 years, Joe has been helping San Antonio families navigate probate and estate planning with straightforward, honest legal service. Born and raised right here in San Antonio, Joe understands the unique needs of Bexar County families and the local courthouse processes that can make or break a probate case.

After graduating from St. Mary's University School of Law -- San Antonio's own law school -- Joe began his career handling complex estate litigation. But he quickly realized something troubling: most families didn't need complex legal strategies. They needed simple, affordable help getting through one of the hardest times in their lives.

That realization changed everything.

  • Education: St. Mary's University School of Law, San Antonio, TX
  • Experience: 15+ years exclusively in Texas probate and estate planning
  • Bar Membership: State Bar of Texas
  • Section Membership: Probate & Estate Planning Section, State Bar of Texas
  • Languages: English and Spanish (fully bilingual)
  • Jurisdiction: All Texas courts, with deep expertise in Bexar County
Our Story

Why I Focus on Simple Probate Only

Early in my career, I worked at a large estate litigation firm. I saw firsthand how families -- already grieving -- were billed thousands of dollars for legal work that could have been handled simply and affordably. Hourly billing turned straightforward probate cases into financial burdens. Families who just needed a will admitted to probate were charged for unnecessary motions, excessive correspondence, and drawn-out timelines.

"I watched a family pay $12,000 to probate a simple will that should have cost a fraction of that. That was the day I decided to do things differently."

I left that firm and opened Joe's Probate Services with one mission: give every San Antonio family access to honest, flat-fee probate. No hourly billing. No surprise invoices. No unnecessary complexity. Just clear, competent legal help when you need it most.

Because I grew up in San Antonio, I know the Bexar County Courthouse inside and out. I know the judges, the clerks, the processes. That local knowledge means your case moves faster, with fewer surprises. Whether you need a muniment of title, a small estate affidavit, or a full independent administration, I handle it with the same care and attention -- because every family deserves that.

Today, we've helped over 2,000 families settle their loved ones' estates and plan for the future. And we do it all with the same simple promise: you'll know exactly what you're paying before we start, and we'll treat your family like our own.
