Probate Application Preparation

Home  /  Services  /  Probate Application Prep

Affordable Option

Professional Documents, Budget-Friendly Price

We understand that not everyone needs — or can afford — full-service probate representation. Some families are comfortable handling things on their own but need help getting the paperwork right. That's exactly what this service is designed for.

With our Probate Application Preparation service, we prepare all the documents you need to file your probate case with the court. You get professionally drafted legal documents — the same quality we use for our full-service clients — at a fraction of the cost.

Think of it as having a professional prepare your documents while you serve as your own representative at the courthouse. We make sure the paperwork is done correctly so you don't face delays or rejections when you file.

This service includes a consultation where we review your situation, determine which type of probate is right for your case, and then prepare all the required documents — the application, any necessary affidavits, proposed orders, and filing instructions. We also provide clear, written guidance on what to do next: where to file, what fees to expect, and how to navigate the court process.

If at any point you decide you'd prefer full representation, we can seamlessly upgrade your service. The amount you've already paid will be credited toward the full-service fee.
