Court filing fees and publication costs are separate from our attorney fees, as they are paid directly to the county and newspaper. In Bexar County, probate filing fees typically range from $300 to $400, and publication costs run around $100 to $200. We'll provide a complete breakdown of all expected costs -- including these third-party fees -- during your free consultation so there are no surprises.
The "from" price represents our starting rate for straightforward cases. Most cases fall at or near the starting price. In some situations -- such as estates with multiple properties, contested matters, or complex family dynamics -- the fee may be higher. We'll always quote your exact fee during your consultation before any work begins, so you know precisely what to expect.
Yes, we offer interest-free payment plans for most services. We understand that legal fees on top of funeral expenses and other costs can be a burden. If unexpected complications arise -- such as a creditor claim, a missing heir, or a contested will -- we'll discuss the situation with you immediately.
